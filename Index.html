What's inside

Everything a freelancer
or small business needs.

Built for the people who just want to track their money — not learn accounting software.

▦
Live Dashboard

At-a-glance view of income, expenses, net profit, pending invoices, and your current running balance — always up to date.

⇄
Transaction Tracking

Log income, expenses, and transfers. Each entry shows a running balance column so you always know where you stand.

⇌
Transfer Support

Record money moving between your own accounts — savings, investments, loan payments — without skewing your P&L.

◫
Invoice Management

Create, track, and mark invoices as paid. See outstanding receivables at a glance and never forget a payment.

▤
Financial Reports

Profit & Loss statement, monthly trend charts, income sources breakdown, and invoice summary — all auto-generated.

◈
Starting Balance

Set your opening balance so your current balance is always accurate, even when you start mid-year or mid-month.

✦
Custom Categories

Use built-in categories or create your own. Custom categories are saved automatically for future transactions.

🔒
100% Private

All data is stored in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to any server. Ever. Your books stay yours.

⚡
Instant Setup

Download one file. Double-click it. Start tracking. No npm install, no account creation, no tutorial required.
